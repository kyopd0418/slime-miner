Game Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to Slime Miner! Here's how to begin your adventure:

  1. Start by digging in the mines to collect slimes
  2. Merge your slimes to create more powerful ones
  3. Trade with other players to get rare slimes
  4. Earn rewards and climb the leaderboard

Mercenary System

Learn how to use mercenaries effectively:

  • Each mercenary has unique skills and abilities
  • Upgrade your mercenaries to increase their power
  • Combine different mercenaries for strategic advantages

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on merging slimes of the same type
  • Save your Cosmo for important upgrades
  • Join the community to learn from other players
  • Check the updates regularly for new features
