5 Easy Steps to Play

1. Start Digging & Upgrade!

  • Upgrade your slimes, drills, engines, coolers, and warehouse to dig deeper and faster
  • The deeper you go, the rarer the minerals you collect!

2. Strategize with Special Slimes!

  • Summon Neo SLs every 4 hours for free
  • Summon Mercenary SLs every 6 hours for free
  • Merge duplicates to level up and boost their effect!

3. Maximize Profits

  • Upgrade Treasure Chest Radar, Offline Mining Boosts, Firefighter Levels, and more
  • Even while you're offline, your slimes keep working for you!

4. Dimensional Expedition

  • Send your Slimes for Expedition to get special materials!

5. Complete Your Daily Tasks!

  • Claim offline rewards & redeem your points
  • Greet your slimes & find the hidden treasure box (left side of the bar)
  • Check upgrade tools & sell extra items for gold
  • Merge SLs and complete missions for bonus rewards
